Solar Power Solutions is a privately owned solar company that was founded in 2012 by two friends, Calvin Phillips and Prince Jaden. We sell and install solar panels to homes and businesses, and we are known for our high-quality solar panels, experienced installation team, and excellent customer service. We have installed solar panels on over 1,000 homes and businesses, and we offer financing options to make solar energy more affordable. We are also a certified B Corporation, which means we meet high standards of social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. If you are interested in learning more about our company or switching to solar energy, please visit our website or give us a call. We would be happy to answer any of your questions and help you get started on your journey to a cleaner, more sustainable future.
